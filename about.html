Our Mission

Empowering Urban youth through digital skills was founded in 2023 with a simple yet powerful mission: Empower unemployed youth with practical skills to achieve independence and economic growth.

Our Story

What started as a small local initiative has grown into a comprehensive leading platform for free vocational education across South Africa and beyond.

Our Values

  • Empowering Urban youth through digital skills: We prioritize the needs and well-being of our youth
  • Inclusivity: We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds and skill levels
  • Impact: We measure our success by the positive change we create
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of Empowering Urban youth through digital skills

Our Team

Our dedicated team consists of experienced community organizers, volunteer coordinators, and passionate individuals who believe in the power of collective action. Together.

Since our founding, we've facilitated over 5,000 hours of volunteer work, supported 50+ local organizations, and connected more than 300 dedicated volunteers with meaningful opportunities. But we're just getting started!
